Our Story

Crafted in Darkness,
Born in Light

A startup born from curiosity, passion and the belief that extraordinary scent should never be out of reach.

The Beginning

From a Kitchen Table
to a Fragrance Brand

Noir & Co started in 2021 in a small apartment in Maseru, where founder Amara Theko began blending essential oils as a creative outlet after years of collecting niche fragrances she could rarely afford. What began as a hobby quickly became a passion — and then a purpose.

Frustrated by luxury perfume prices that excluded everyday people, Amara envisioned a brand that could deliver genuine artisan quality at an honest price point. With a background in chemistry and a lifelong love of scent, she began developing unique fragrance profiles that told stories.

After months of testing and feedback from friends and family, the first three Noir & Co fragrances launched online in late 2022 — selling out within 48 hours. The response was overwhelming and humbling.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We source ingredients responsibly and use recyclable, minimal packaging. Our supply chain is transparent and environmentally conscious at every stage.

Cruelty-Free

100% vegan formulas, never tested on animals. We believe beauty should never come at the cost of another living creature — full stop.

Authenticity

No synthetic filler, no mass-market shortcuts. Each fragrance reflects genuine artistry — developed with patience and respect for tradition.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

2020 — The Idea is Born

An Apartment. A Dream. A Lab.

Thenzane begins experimenting with fragrance blends in her Maseru apartment, inspired by a desire to make artisan perfumery accessible to all.

2022 — First Collection Launched

Sold Out in 48 Hours

Three debut fragrances go live on the Noir & Co online store and sell out completely within two days of launch.

2023 — Team Expands & Brand Grows

A Real Company Takes Shape

Lebo and Senate join the company. The product line expands to 10 scents. Noir & Co is featured in an international lifestyle magazine for the first time.

2024 — 12,000 Customers & Counting

Crossing Borders

Noir & Co ships to four countries. A flagship discovery sample kit launches. The first wholesale partnerships are signed with boutiques in Johannesburg and Nairobi.

2025 — New Collections & Partnerships

The Lumière Dorée Era

The Lumière Dorée seasonal collection launches to critical acclaim. Wholesale distribution expands to London. Investor conversations begin.
