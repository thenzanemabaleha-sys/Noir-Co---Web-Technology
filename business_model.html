How We Operate

The Noir & Co
Business Model

A sustainable, direct-to-consumer fragrance brand built on creativity, transparency, and a deep respect for craft.

Business Model Canvas

Our Strategic Framework

Below are the two most important layers of our business model: who we work with and what we offer, and how we spend and earn.

1 Key Partners

Who We Work With

  • Ethical raw ingredient suppliers (Bulgaria, India, Morocco)
  • Independent boutique retail partners
  • Eco-friendly glass bottle manufacturers
  • Logistics and fulfilment partners
  • Digital marketing & creative agencies
2 Key Activities

What We Do

  • Fragrance R&D and small-batch production
  • Direct-to-consumer e-commerce
  • Brand storytelling and content marketing
  • Customer community building
  • Wholesale relationship management
3 Value Propositions

What We Offer

  • Artisan quality at accessible prices
  • 100% vegan and cruelty-free formulas
  • Narrative-driven, unique scent profiles
  • Transparent ingredient sourcing
  • Discovery kits to reduce purchase risk
4 Cost Structure

Our Main Costs

  • Raw material procurement
  • Small-batch manufacturing
  • Premium packaging design & production
  • E-commerce platform fees
  • Paid digital advertising
  • Fulfilment and international shipping
  • Salaries and contractor fees
  • R&D for new collections
5 Revenue Streams

How We Earn

  • Direct product sales (full-size bottles)
  • Sample and discovery kit sales
  • Gift set bundles (premium margin)
  • Wholesale to boutique partners
  • Seasonal limited-edition releases
How We Make Money

Revenue Streams

Direct E-Commerce Sales

Our primary channel — customers shop directly through our website, capturing full margin on every sale.

Wholesale to Boutiques

We supply select independent boutiques in Johannesburg, Nairobi, and London at negotiated pricing.

Gift Sets & Discovery Kits

Curated bundles and sample kits drive new customer acquisition and raise average order value.

Limited Edition Releases

7%of revenue
