Signature Scent

Minuit Noir

Black Pepper · Midnight Rose · Incense · Oud · Vetiver

It was past midnight in Maseru when Amara Theko first got it right. She had spent six months chasing a single feeling — the stillness that falls over a city at 2 a.m., when the air carries the memory of everything that happened that day. On that final attempt, she blended dark oud with a whisper of midnight rose and a trail of smoky vetiver, and the moment the fragrance settled on her wrist, she knew. Minuit Noir isn't just a perfume; it is a whole atmosphere — bold enough to announce your arrival and mysterious enough to make people wonder long after you've left the room.
